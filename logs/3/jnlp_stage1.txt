Notes:

- BSARD: moderate improvement over BM25
- IL-PCSR: regression vs BM25; root cause: extreme query/doc length (avg query 3396 words,
  avg doc 716 words, max 43600 words) causes BGE-M3 embeddings to compress into
  generic vectors with near-zero separability; BM25 benefits from raw token overlap
  on long text; also 5017 train queries / 936 docs = BM25 top-100 negatives cover
  10.7% of entire corpus, reducing hard negative informativeness
- STARD: regression vs BM25; Chinese corpus is large (55348 docs) and BGE-M3
  product features may not discriminate well across such a large corpus
